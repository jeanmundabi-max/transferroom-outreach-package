12-Week Roadmap

Four phases. One system. Owned at Day 90.

Phase 1 — Weeks 1 to 3

Build the universe

  • Expand 28-club starter to 150+ qualified contacts
  • Head of Analysis, Data Scientist, Director of Football roles
  • Cross-reference against TransferRoom's existing client list
  • Tier 1 (confirmed buying signals): 50 to 75 contacts
  • Tier 2 (adjacent signals): 75 to 100 contacts
  • Validate contact data — verify employment status before deploy
Phase 2 — Weeks 4 to 6

Launch outreach — Trigger angle

  • Email 1 (Trigger, PSS 87) deployed to Tier 1 contacts
  • LinkedIn connection sequence runs in parallel
  • Open rate monitoring — daily for first week
  • Email 2 (Friction, PSS 85) sent Day 3 to non-responders
  • First qualified conversations begin — transferred to Neal's team
  • Reply rate benchmark: target 3% to 5% on first 50 sends
Phase 3 — Weeks 7 to 9

Qualify, refine, and expand

  • Performance review: which segments (Championship vs Bundesliga 2) respond best
  • Which email angles drive replies — adjust sequencing based on data
  • Expand to Tier 2 contacts with refined targeting
  • Launch parallel Women's Marketplace sequence
  • First trial initiations expected from Tier 1 clubs
  • HubSpot nurture sequence live for all non-converted contacts
Phase 4 — Weeks 10 to 12

System handover — TransferRoom owns it

  • Full targeting database (150+ contacts, tiered, with notes)
  • Email sequence templates — tested against live audience
  • Qualification framework — three-criteria scoring rubric
  • LinkedIn content playbook — 20 briefs for analytics function audience
  • 20-week nurture sequence — ready to run independently
  • Performance benchmarks: open rate, reply rate, qualified rate
