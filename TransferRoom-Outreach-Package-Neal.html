Missing Buyer Persona

One buyer persona away from a second revenue line

TransferRoom's commercial motion reaches Directors of Football. It does not reach the analytics function. These are different people, different conferences, different peer networks.

Who the marketplace reaches

  • Director of Football — transfer decisions, budget holder
  • Sporting Director — squad planning, agent relationships
  • Club COO and CEO — commercial and partnership decisions
  • Senior agents — deal facilitation, player availability
  • Found at: Soccerex, World Football Summit, Sportel, Football Finance Forum

Who buys data products (not being reached)

  • Head of Analysis — evaluates and recommends data tools
  • Data Scientist — runs proof of concepts, manages integrations
  • Performance Analyst — primary daily user of data products
  • Chief Data Officer (larger clubs) — data stack strategy and budget
  • Found at: StatsBomb Conference, PFSA events, LinkedIn, Discord communities, not Soccerex

The Director of Football approves the data product budget. The Head of Analysis runs the proof of concept and makes the recommendation. For TransferRoom to win data product deals, it needs to enter the Head of Analysis conversation — not just the Director of Football conversation. MDB builds the channel to reach this buyer directly.
