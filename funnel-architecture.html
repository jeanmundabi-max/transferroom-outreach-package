Top of Funnel
  • Soccerex and World Football Summit — 2 events per year
  • Word of mouth from existing marketplace clients
  • Conference reach: ~10 to 15% of relevant analytics buyer market
  • Analytics function at clubs — not in this audience
  • Targeted email outreach to Head of Analysis at 150+ clubs
  • LinkedIn content flywheel reaching analytics function (20 briefs)
  • Ownership change trigger — outreach within 30 days of announcement
  • Analyst hire trigger — new Head of Analysis = tool evaluation window
  • Content targeting Head of Analysis persona on LinkedIn and blog
  • Proactive outreach to analyst communities (StatsBomb, PFSA)
  • Automated ownership change monitoring and rapid outreach
  • Women's Marketplace dedicated outbound channel
Middle of Funnel
  • Demo requests from clubs that already know about the product
  • Evaluations happen only when club reaches out first
  • No qualification framework — all demos treated equally
  • Coverage: only clubs already in TransferRoom's marketplace network
  • Three-criteria qualification: budget, use case, timeline
  • MDB qualifies replies — only intent-verified contacts transferred
  • 20-week nurture sequence for clubs in the "not yet ready" bucket
  • Pre-call brief and agenda-setting for all qualified demos
  • Nurture sequence for clubs that engaged but did not convert
  • Qualification framework to sort curious from ready-to-buy
  • Retargeting via LinkedIn Ads to club data professionals
  • Case study content to accelerate evaluation phase
Bottom of Funnel
  • Consultative proof of concept with clubs that request a demo
  • Product converts well when it reaches this stage — strong signals
  • Tiago Pinto (ex-AS Roma GM) testimonial confirms enterprise adoption
  • Problem: the funnel only fills from clubs that already found TransferRoom
  • More qualified clubs enter proof of concept from MDB outreach
  • Each signed club becomes a case study for the next cohort
  • Peer referral from early data product clients accelerates adoption
  • TransferRoom commercial team enters each evaluation early — before shortlist forms
  • Competitor comparison page (TransferRoom data vs Wyscout vs StatsBomb)
  • Proof of concept framework for data product demos specifically
  • Structured upsell pathway from data product to full platform suite
Post-Sale
  • MLS multi-year extension — strong renewal example
  • Tiago Pinto testimonial suggests product satisfaction is high
  • No systematic upsell from marketplace to data product or vice versa
  • Case study documentation after each signed club: club name, metric, window result
  • Structured upsell: data product client → marketplace upgrade → Women's Marketplace
  • Referral ask at 90-day mark — "Which Head of Analysis in your peer group has the same problem?"
  • Renewal playbook with proactive value delivery before contract renewal
  • Net Revenue Retention tracking for data product clients
Three Growth Loops

Each loop reinforces the next. Not a one-time campaign.

Conference acquisition → data product upsell → club referral

↓

↓

↓

↓

Loop 2 — Ownership Change Trigger

↓
Outreach to incoming Sporting Director within 30 days
↓
Data stack audit → TransferRoom data product signed
↓
Case study published → shared with next ownership-change club
↓
Peer referral among ownership networks → accelerates adoption
Loop 3 — Test, Measure, Scale

Each 90-day cycle more efficient than the last

↓
Data: which segments respond (Championship vs Bundesliga 2)
↓
Which angles drive replies (Trigger vs Friction)
↓
Refine targeting → next 90-day cycle
↓
Each cycle more efficient — compounding advantage
